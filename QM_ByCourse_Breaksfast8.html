Gourmet Salad Flatbread
Ingredients

1 whole wheat or sprouted wheat tortilla, toasted until crisp
2 wedges of Laughing Cow Light Garlic Cheese
1 oz (a few thin slices) of organic pepper jack cheese
3 fresh romaine lettuce leaves
1 tomato, sliced
A handful of microgreens
Salt & pepper

Directions

Toast the tortilla until crisp, spread with the laughing cow cheese wedges and then top with the organic pepper jack cheese slices while still warm (so the cheeses melt together)

Top with romaine leaves, season with salt & pepper. Top with tomato slices, season again with salt & pepper. Top with microgreens.
