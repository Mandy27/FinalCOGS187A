Welcome

We want to help Tritons find the resources they need to cook for themselves. Many students come to UCSD with little or no cooking experience, but on this site we have compiled plenty of easy recipes to try out, even considering dietary and time restrictions. By giving Tritons useful tools on how to prepare their own meals, we hope to encourage them to cook for themselves and their friends more often.
