Welcome

This website helps Tritons find the resources they need to cook for themselves. Our site includes a variety of recipes to explore, information on how to maintain a balanced diet and stay within a budget when shopping for food, and even tips on where to shop for food in the La Jolla area. By giving Tritons useful tools on how to prepare their own meals, we hope to encourage them to cook for themselves and their friends more often.
Recipies

Explore recipes by cuisine, by a specific course or by occasion, find budget friendly options, and check out our handy ingredient reference guide!

Healthy choice

Find recipes that are gluten-free and vegetarian, diet tips, meal planners, and calorie counters.

Quick Meals

Watch some cooking videos for tips, find a recipe by ingredients you already have, check out a weekly meal planner for cooking ahead of time, and for those who just need food now check out delivery places in La Jolla.
