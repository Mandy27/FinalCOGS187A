Useful Links

Where to buy

Here you can find information on grocery stores in La Jolla, all of the Farmers Markets in San Diego County, and a list and map of UCSD Campus markets.

Search by Ingredients

This site will ask for what ingredients you have available at home, but they will also show you some example ingredients most kitchens already have in case you forget the basics. As you add more ingredients recipes will appear for which you have everything needed. You can also search for recipes by name or category if you already have an idea of what to make with what you have at home.

Meal Planner

On this website you can explore a variety of premade meal plans that you can customize by the number of servings you will be preparing. You can find grocery store savings by the grocery store you visit most often. The plans focus on dinners but you can optionally add lunch, breakfast, and even dessert plans.

Ingredients Reference Guide

This website contains a cooking encyclopedia that covers thousands of ingredients and kitchen tools where you can find pictures, descriptions, synonyms, pronunciations, and even suggested substitutions should you not be able to obtain an ingredient! Often they will give you tips on how to store ingredients so feel free to visit this site often if you are buying new foods that you are less familiar with.

Calorie Counter

This website provides a customizable calorie counter tool in order to help you maintain your weight by estimating how many calories your body needs per day. It takes into account your age, current weight, height, gender, and even your activity level! Should you want to lose weight, it will even give you advice on how to reach your goal weight.

Food ToGo

This website will find restaurants that deliver to you based on your address. You can also browse restaurants that deliver in your area by neighborhood or cuisine. Once you find a restaurant that you are interested in, you can view their menu and online coupons and discounts, read reviews by other users of the site, and order online for free.
