Granola-Yogurt Parfait
Ingredients

1/2 cup(s) fresh or frozen (partially thawed) raspberries or other favorite berry
3/4 cup(s) vanilla low-fat yogurt
2 tablespoon(s) low-fat granola

Directions

Into parfait glass or wineglass, spoon some raspberries, vanilla yogurt, and granola. Repeat layering until all ingredients are used.
